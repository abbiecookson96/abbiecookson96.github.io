Build a website that works.

I am a front-end developer & UX designer based in Pontefract, West Yorkshire. I am creative, self-motivated and I have a keen eye for all things design. Studying Web Technologies at university has given me a solid foundation and better understanding of visual communication and the digital design process. I am confident using UX processes, and have proven to have strong front-end development skills. Working 12 months as a Front-end Developer at Ebuyer.com, I interpreted client requirements and developed concepts to suit their needs and tight deadlines. It was an excellent opportunity to get real hands on experience in a challenging work environment. My ultimate goal is to build a career within the front-end development and UX design industry.
